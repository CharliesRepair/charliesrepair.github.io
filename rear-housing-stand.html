--- title: "Rear housing stand" layout: pages ---

Rear housing stand for tractors adjustable from 13 inches to 21 inches. Stand attaches to drawbar for complete transmission overhaul and/or axle removal. With adapter plate this stand will also support the 5 speed transmission.

806 differential case on Rear Housing Stand
Rear Housing Stand fitted to a John Deere 4630
The rear housing stand fits on a 656 Hydro.
This is a picture of a Farmall 450 differential case that I bolted to the Rear Housing stand.
Here is an adapter plate that I designed to fasten to the PTO opening bolt holes when doing a major repair of a Farmall H differential case.
This is a picture of the front support holding up the differential case.
The adapter plate can also be used when the U-shaped drawbar is left on the tractor. Using this adapter keeps the stand more towards the front of the differential case.
