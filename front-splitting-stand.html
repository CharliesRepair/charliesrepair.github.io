--- title: "Front splitting stand" layout: pages ---

5 inch steel casters with roller bearings for easy rolling allows the engine of the tractor to be rolled forward. This stand fits most all tractors with side rails. Easy to install, only requires four bolts. This stand weighs in at about 75 lbs.
