The Hitachi alternator is a small size alternator and fits under the hood on the Farmall H and 300/350 series of tractor. These alternators are rated at 12 volts 35 amps. One thing that I'm very impressed with is the fact that these alternators start charging at very low RPMs. I have done all the home work on these kits. They come with a mounting bracket, tightner bracket, new alternator with the correct size pulley. The kits also have a wiring kit that includes a diode that prevents alternator feedback, making the instulation very easy. All of the Hitachi kits use the original generator belt to run the alternator.
