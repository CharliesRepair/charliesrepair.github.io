This stand provides support of the rear frame when making a rear split or when removal of wheels and axle carriers are required. The adjustable version of rear housing stand was designed to fit the larger rear housing found on the larger John Deere tractors (46xx 47xx 48xx & 49xx series of tractors). The front leg of this stand telescopes forward an additional 25 inches this helps to make this stand much more versatile. When not in use the stand stores on end for easy storage.
